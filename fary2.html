RECIPES

1) CHICKEN SAMOSA:

* 1/2 kg chicken ka keema lein. Usmein 1 cup barik kata hua pyaz aur patta gobhi (aadha pao ke kareeb) ache se milayein. Namak apne taste ke mutabik, ghar ka garam masala, aur thoda tandoori masala daal kar achhe se mix karlein.

* Ab ghar ki bani hui ya readymade samosa pati lein. Mix ko samosa pati mein bhar ke samosa bana lein. Ab aap inhe 1 mahine ke liye freeze kar sakte hain ya turant fry karke garam-garam serve kar sakte hain. Imli ki chutney ya tamatar ki sauce ke saath maze karein.

IN ENGLISH :

Chicken Samosa: * Take 1/2 kg minced chicken. Add 1 cup finely chopped onions and finely shredded cabbage (around half a quarter). Mix it well. Add salt as per taste, homemade garam masala, and a little tandoori masala. Combine everything thoroughly.
* Use either homemade samosa sheets or readymade ones. Fill the mixture into the samosa sheets and make samosas. At this point, you can either freeze them for up to 1 month or fry them immediately. Serve hot with tamarind chutney or tomato sauce.

2) Seekh Paratha:

A) Seekh:

* 1/2 kg chicken ya mutton ke boneless pieces
* 1/2 tsp homemade garam masala
* Bazaar ka seekh masala, namak, aur chili flakes – sab kuch ache se mixer mein mix karlo.
* Ek pyaaz finely chop karo (dhyan rakho ki usme bilkul bhi paani na ho).
* Bread crumbs add karke sab kuch ache se mix karo.
* Thoda sa ghee ya butter binding ke liye add karo.
* * Ab is mixture ko seekh ka shape do aur thodi der ke liye fridge mein set hone ke liye rakh do. Is point par aap seekhs ko 1 mahine ke liye freeze kar sakte ho. Jab banane ka mann ho, toh pan mein fry karlo ya tandoor mein roast karlo. Pudine aur pyaaz ke lachhon ke saath serve karo.

B) Paratha:

* Maida (all-purpose flour) mein doodh, ek anda, thoda sa namak aur sugar add karke ache se aata gundh lo aur isse 1 ghante ke liye dhak kar rest karne do.
* Phir parathe bel lo.
* Tawa par tandoori style sekho ya tel mein fry karlo.
* Aapke seekh parathe ready hain! Pudine ki chutney aur pyaaz ke saath enjoy karo. Nosh farmaiye!

IN ENGLISH :

Seekh Ingredients: * 1/2 kg chicken or mutton boneless pieces
* 1/2 tsp homemade garam masala
* Bazaar ka seekh masala, salt, and chili flakes – mix everything well in a mixer.
* Chop one onion finely (make sure there is no water in it).
* Add bread crumbs and mix everything properly.
* Add a little ghee or butter for binding.
* Shape the mixture into seekhs and leave them in the fridge for a while to set. At this point, you can freeze them for up to a month. When ready, either fry them in a pan or roast them in a tandoor. Serve with mint and onion rings.
** Paratha:
* In maida (all-purpose flour), add milk, one egg, a pinch of salt, and a little sugar. Knead the dough well and let it rest, covered, for 1 hour.
* Roll out the parathas.
* Cook them on a tawa (griddle) like tandoori parathas or fry them in oil.
* Your seekh paratha is ready! Serve with mint chutney and onion rings. Enjoy!

3) Shami Kabab:

Shami Kabab Ingredients:

* 1/2 kg chana dal
* 1/2 kg mutton or chicken boneless pieces
* Adrak (ginger) and lehsun (garlic) as per taste
* 1 tsp garam masala
* 2 onions (finely chopped)
* 4 green chilies

Method:

* Sab kuch (all ingredients) ko ache se cook karo, jab tak dal aur meat soft ho jaye.
* Phir is mixture ko mash karlo aur ek smooth texture me layao.
* Is mashed mixture ki medium-sized tikkiyan banao.
* Tikkiyan ko fridge me set hone ke liye rakh do. Agar baad me banani ho, toh inhe 1 mahine ke liye freeze kar sakte ho.
* Jab kabab fry karne ka mann ho, tikkiyan ko ande me dip karo aur shallow fry karlo.
* Shami Kabab tayar hain! Inhe green chutney aur sauce ke saath serve karke enjoy karo!

IN ENGLISH :

Shami Kabab: Ingredients: * 1/2 kg chana dal (split chickpeas) * 1/2 kg mutton or chicken boneless pieces * Ginger and garlic (as per taste) * 1 tsp garam masala * 2 onions (finely chopped) * 4 green chilies ** Method: * Cook all the ingredients together until the dal and meat become tender. * Mash the mixture into a smooth paste. * Shape the mashed mixture into medium-sized patties. * Place the patties in the fridge to set. You can also freeze them for up to 1 month at this stage. * When ready to cook, dip the patties in egg and shallow fry them. * Your Shami Kababs are ready! Serve them with green chutney and sauce, and enjoy!
