TIPS & TRICKS

"Welcome to the Cooking Tips & Tricks Section!" Cooking doesn’t have to be complicated! Here, you’ll find quick and handy tips to make your time in the kitchen easier, faster, and more enjoyable. Whether it’s peeling garlic effortlessly or saving an over-salted soup, these small hacks can make a big difference in your cooking routine. Let’s simplify and elevate your culinary experience together!

** Peel Garlic Easily: Place garlic cloves in a jar, close the lid, and shake vigorously. The skins will loosen, making peeling effortless.

** Prevent Pot Overflow: Place a wooden spoon across the top of a boiling pot to prevent it from bubbling over.

** Soften Butter Quickly: Grate cold butter or cut it into small cubes to soften it faster.

** Perfect Hard-Boiled Eggs: Add a pinch of salt or a splash of vinegar to the water to make peeling easier after boiling.

** Keep Lemons Juicy: Roll lemons on the counter with firm pressure before cutting to release more juice.

** Save Over-Salted Soups: Add a raw potato to absorb excess salt, then remove it once the soup tastes balanced.

** Cheese grate karna asaan: Cheese ko grate karne se pehle 10-15 minutes ke liye freezer mein rakh lo. Sticky nahi hoga aur easily grate hoga.

** Bananas jaldi ripen karo: Bananas ko ek brown paper bag mein ek apple ke saath rakho. Ye ripening process fast kar deta hai.

** Juicy burgers banao: Burger patties ke beech mein chhota sa dent bana do, isse evenly cook hoti hai aur puff nahi hoti.

** Onions ka effect kam karo: Chop karne se pehle onions ko kuch minutes ke liye freezer mein rakh lo, tears cause karne wale enzymes release nahi hote.

** Fruits fresh rakho: Apples aur bananas jese sliced fruits ko lemon juice mein toss kar lo, discoloration nahi hoga.

** Pizza ko reheat karo: Leftover pizza ko low heat pe skillet par rakho, crust crispy rehega aur soggy nahi hoga.

** Eggshell pieces hatao: Wet fingertip se small eggshell pieces ko asaani se bowl se utha lo.

** Sticky rice avoid karo: Rice ko cook karne se pehle thande paani mein achhe se rinse kar lo taaki excess starch nikal jaaye.

** Ginger ka peel hatao: Knife ke bajaye ek spoon ke edge se ginger ka skin scrape karo. Easy aur safe hai.

** Cutting board ko steady rakho: Cutting board ke neeche ek damp towel rakho taaki woh slide na kare.
