4) Frozen Paratha:

* Maida (all-purpose flour) aur gehun ka aata mix karlo, ya dono ko barabar quantity mein milalo.
* Add namak, sugar, 2 ande (eggs), aur doodh. Isse ache se gundh lo aur 1 ghante ke liye dhak kar rest karne do.
* Phir apne pasand ke mutabik patle ya mote parathe bel lo.
* Parathon ko tawa par half-done karlo.
* Sab parathe isi tarah tayar karlo.
* Thande hone ke baad parathon ko ek ke upar ek stack karo, lekin har ek ke beech mein butter paper ya plastic ka kata hua sheet rakh lo, taaki freeze hone ke baad parathe aapas mein chipke na.
* In stacked parathon ko ziplock bag mein rakho aur freeze karlo.
* Jab khane ka mann ho, to tawa par ghee ya tel mein ache se pakalo.
* Aapke Frozen Parathe tayar hain! Inhe apni pasand ki chutney ya accompaniment ke saath enjoy karo.

IN ENGLISH :

Frozen Paratha:* Take all-purpose flour or wheat flour, or mix both in equal quantities. * Add salt, sugar, 2 eggs, and milk. Knead the dough well and let it rest, covered, for 1 hour. * Then roll out parathas as thin or thick as you prefer. * Cook the parathas on a griddle until they are half-done. * Prepare all parathas in the same way. * Once cooled, stack them one on top of the other, placing butter paper or cut plastic sheets in between each paratha to prevent them from sticking while freezing. * Store the stacked parathas in a ziplock bag and freeze them. * When you're ready to eat, cook them thoroughly on a griddle with ghee or oil. * Your Frozen Parathas are ready! Serve them with your choice of chutney or accompaniment and enjoy!

5)Frozen Tomatoes:

* Tamatar ko dhoke bina pani ke cooker mein daalo aur ek seeti (one whistle) le lo.
* Thanda hone ke baad, sab ko mixer mein ache se blend kar lo.
* Is puree ko apne pasand ke ice trays ya kisi bhi container mein daal kar freezer mein set hone ke liye rakh do.
* Jab puree ache se freeze ho jaye (approximately 3 ghante mein), usse ice cubes ki tarah nikaal kar ziplock bags mein pack kar lo aur fir se freezer mein rakh do. Is tarah yeh cubes aapas mein chipkenge nahi.
* Jab bhi cooking mein zarurat ho, ek ya do cubes gravy mein daal do.
* Yeh cubes asani se mix ho jate hain aur dish ka taste do guna kar dete hain. Cooking bhi easy ban jati hai.
* Jab tamatar mehenga ho jate hain, tab yeh stored puree kaafi bachat karwati hai. Aap ise 2 mahine tak araam se use kar sakte ho.

IN ENGLISH :

Frozen Tomatoes:* Wash the tomatoes and put them in a pressure cooker without adding water. Take one whistle. * Once cooled, blend everything well in a mixer to make a puree. * Pour the puree into ice trays or any suitable container and keep it in the freezer to set. * Once frozen properly (in about 3 hours), remove the cubes and pack them in ziplock bags. Place them back in the freezer. This way, the cubes wonâ€™t stick together. * Whenever needed, add one or two cubes to your gravy while cooking. * These cubes mix easily into the dish and double the flavor. It also makes cooking more convenient. * Storing this puree is cost-effective, especially when tomatoes are expensive. You can use it comfortably for up to 2 months.

6)Chicken Lollipop:

* Chicken ke leg pieces ko ache se wash karlo aur channi mein rakh ke thodi der chhod do, taaki excess water nikal jaye.
* Phir unmein thodi malai ya dahi, red chili powder, 1 tsp soy sauce, 1 tsp chili sauce, namak, aur 1 tsp adrak-lehsun paste daal do.
* Sab kuch ache se mix karlo.
* Is stage par, aap isse ek mahine ke liye freezer mein rakh sakte ho.
* Ya phir mixture ko 1 ghante ke liye chhod do, aur baad mein is par halka cornflour sprinkle karke fry karlo.
* Fry hone ke baad, chat masala aur sauce ke saath serve karo aur enjoy karo!

IN ENGLISH :

Chicken Lollipop: * Thoroughly wash the chicken leg pieces and let them drain in a strainer for a while to remove excess water. * Then, marinate the chicken with some cream or yogurt, red chili powder, 1 tsp soy sauce, 1 tsp chili sauce, salt, and 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste. * Mix everything well. * At this stage, you can freeze it for up to one month. * Alternatively, let the marinated chicken rest for 1 hour, then sprinkle some cornflour on it and fry. * Once fried, serve with chaat masala and your favorite sauce. Enjoy!
